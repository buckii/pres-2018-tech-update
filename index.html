Themes for 2018

Democratization

Advanced tech isn't just for techies anymore. Kids, seniors, small business, government... everyone can have it.

Omnipresence

You can't escape tech. Get your information and services anywhere and everywhere—whether you like it or not.

Accessibility

Separate but equal is not okay. Tech today is built with impairments and alternative UI's in mind.

Privacy & Security

Ubiquitous tech is as accessible to bad actors as it is to intended users. Some people are concerned.

Modernization

Old companies, institutions, and people are getting onboard with modern tech so they aren't left behind.
